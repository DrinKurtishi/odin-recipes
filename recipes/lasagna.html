Lasagna recipe


Description: Pasta speciality from Italy origin.

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base.
Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly!
This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 ounce squashed tomato
  • 1 tablespoon italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles

Steps:

  1. Mix the olive oil with the beef
  2. Grind onion and caramelize it
  3. Add in the tomatoes to the onions
  4. Add italian seasoning and vinegar
  5. Mix everything together and that's your sauce!
  6. Put one sheet of lasagna noodle on tray
  7. Sprinkle in cheese and add the lasagna mix
  8. Repeat 10 times
  9. Bake in oven for 1 hour at 225 degrees
  10. Enjoy!
