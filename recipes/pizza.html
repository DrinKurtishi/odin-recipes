Pizza recipe

Description: Italy's biggest contribution! The every tasty pizza!

Make perfect pizza at home with this classic homemade pizza recipe,
including a pizza dough recipe, topping suggestions, and step-by-step instructions

Ingredients:

  • Olive Oil
  • Tomate sauce
  • Pizza dough
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Feta cheese
  • Toppings (sausage, olives)
  • Baby aragula

Steps:

  1. Unfreeze pizza dough
  2. Spread tomato sauce on dough
  3. Add cheese
  4. Add toppings
  5. Add more cheese
  6. Bake at 220 degrees for 20 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
